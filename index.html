Synopsis

A catastrophic malfunction causes Astro Scientist Julia Tovey's space station to break up and she narrowly escapes the wreckage. Alone and floating through the stars in a life pod for one, Julia devises a plan to make her way back to Earth and to her family. But when the pod is struck by a freak asteroid storm affecting her oxygen supply, her newly found engineering skills are pushed to their limit. With time running out, Julia must face her fears or die alone in space.
